About me

My name is Zuzana. I am a woman, a mother of two sons, and a guide through a woman's inner world on her journey back to herself. I love yoga, dance, nature, and beauty in all its forms. I am fascinated by the depths of the human psyche and the spiritual dimensions of our being, and I look for ways to bridge them with the material world so they can support each other and create a harmonious whole.

Around the time my second son was born, I began to undergo a deep awakening and my own inner transformation. During the following years, I went through burnout, the end of my marriage, and the complete collapse of my life as I had known it until then. I was forced to feel a multitude of heavy emotions that I had previously avoided and to re-evaluate my approach to myself and to life itself. I had to admit where I hadn't been living truly in alignment with my truth. I began to accept all my long-suppressed parts, trying to understand and integrate them.

Throughout this process, I sought help and tried various methods of inner healing, self-development, and conscious manifestation... The Journey Therapy proved to be the most effective and comprehensive for me. Thanks to it, I more clearly realized and processed traumas from my childhood as well as from the deeper past of my soul. I understood many karmic connections. I discovered negative beliefs that had been subtly influencing my reality without me even realizing it. I came to understand that my old life fell apart because it wasn't in alignment with who I truly am. I also realized that the outer world is a reflection of the inner one, and if I want to change something on the outside, I must first change it within myself.

About The Journey Method

The Journey Therapy was developed by the internationally acclaimed therapist, teacher, and author, Brandon Bays. It originated from her own life experience, where she managed to heal a tumor the size of a basketball within 7 weeks. She describes her story in the book The Journey. It is a simple but highly effective technique based on releasing old emotional wounds and initiating the body's self-healing process.

It is scientifically proven that emotions trigger biochemical reactions in our bodies. When they are suppressed, cellular receptors become blocked, disrupting healthy communication between cells. This creates a disorder in the body, which is initially hidden but can eventually develop into an illness precisely at the site of these blocked cellular receptors. A large part of these blockages originates in early childhood and manifests both as a predisposition to certain health problems and as negative psychological programming—we carry various conclusions and beliefs from unprocessed traumatic experiences that then impede the natural flow of our lives and the fulfillment of our potential.

During therapy, there is a re-experiencing and release of suppressed emotions and the uncovering of related cellular memories, which are the root cause of the client's difficulties. These memories are processed in a way that leads to inner reconciliation and forgiveness. The client simultaneously finds a path to a deep inner experience of peace, unity, love, and fulfillment.

My offer

I offer my clients, above all, a safe environment filled with acceptance and support. My priority is to create a space where it is possible to relax, build trust, and open up as deeply as possible to the inner transformation process. I use The Journey therapy as my primary tool. Using this method, we will:

  • Discover memories of life events that caused emotional distortion
  • Release blocked emotions and process traumatic events to reach a state of reconciliation
  • Uncover and clear negative beliefs that were formed during these experiences
  • Create new, positive, and liberating beliefs
  • Allow ourselves to experience deep relaxation and unconditional self-acceptance while connecting to our inner Source

One session lasts approximately 2.5 to 3 hours. The first session is preceded by a short video call, during which we can introduce ourselves, see if our energies resonate, and discuss the therapeutic process in more detail. There will also be space for your questions. This initial call is free of charge. The therapy itself usually takes place online, but it is also possible to arrange a face-to-face meeting in Prague.
